press arrow keys, 1-6, pgup, pgdn, home and end keys to see what happens.

shape change:
home > turn into circle
end > turn into cube

camera pan (fixed position but orientation change)
1 > left
2 > right
3 > up
4 > down
5 > clockwise
6 > anti-clockwise

camera shift
up arrow > closer to origin
down arrow > farther from origin

imagine camera with its own axis
page up > straight up
page down > straight down
left arrow > straight left
right arrow > straight right
